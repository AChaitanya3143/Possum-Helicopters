Our Story

Where passion meets
precision

From a workshop filled with vintage photographs and aged leather to a world-class facility crafting the future of luxury aviation.

The Dream

The dream that
started it all

In a small workshop filled with vintage aviation photographs and the scent of aged leather, three aerospace engineers asked a question that would change luxury aviation forever: "What if helicopters could be beautiful again?"

It was 2023. Modern helicopters had become purely functional — efficient machines devoid of character. Meanwhile, vintage aircraft sat in museums, admired but relegated to history.

We refused to accept that choice. Two years of relentless work later, Possum Helicopters took flight.
